# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Idea-Luminate

### 1. **Notion API**
   - **Rationale**: Notion is widely used by indie hackers for project management and idea organization. Integrating with Notion allows users to seamlessly transfer generated ideas into their Notion workspace for further development.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Up to 1,000 blocks per workspace.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances user workflow by enabling easy idea tracking and organization.

### 2. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Zapier connects Idea-Luminate with thousands of apps, automating workflows for users. This integration can help indie hackers streamline their processes, saving time and effort.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 100 tasks per month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Automates idea validation workflows, allowing users to focus on execution.

### 3. **Trello API**
   - **Rationale**: Trello is a popular tool for task management among indie hackers. Integrating with Trello can help users visualize their ideas and track progress.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited cards, but limited to 10 boards.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Facilitates idea management and prioritization.

### 4. **Slack API**
   - **Rationale**: Many indie hackers use Slack for team communication. Integrating with Slack allows users to receive notifications and updates about their generated ideas directly in their communication channels.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 5,000 messages searchable.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances collaboration and real-time feedback on ideas.

### 5. **Google Sheets API**
   - **Rationale**: Google Sheets is commonly used for data organization and analysis. This integration allows users to export their ideas into a spreadsheet for further analysis and validation.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 15 GB of storage across Google Drive.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies data management and analysis for idea validation.

### 6. **Stripe**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating with Stripe allows users to monetize their ideas directly through Idea-Luminate, providing a seamless payment solution for any services or products they create.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: No monthly fees; transaction fees apply.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Enables users to validate their ideas through real revenue generation.

### 7. **Airtable API**
   - **Rationale**: Airtable combines the simplicity of a spreadsheet with the power of a database. This integration can help users manage their ideas and projects more effectively.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 1,200 records per base.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides a flexible way to organize and track ideas and projects.

### 8. **Figma API**
   - **Rationale**: Figma is a popular design tool among indie hackers. Integrating with Figma allows users to create mockups or prototypes of their ideas directly from Idea-Luminate.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Up to 3 projects.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
   - **Value-Add**: Streamlines the design process, enabling faster prototyping of ideas.

## Prioritization
1. Notion API
2. Zapier
3. Trello API
4. Slack API
5. Google Sheets API
6. Stripe
7. Airtable API
8. Figma API

Focus on partnerships that provide affiliate or revenue-sharing opportunities, particularly with Stripe and Zapier, to enhance monetization potential for both Idea-Luminate and its users.
