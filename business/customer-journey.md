 # Customer Journey Map - idea-luminate

## Aware

### Trigger Event
Indie hacker encounters a challenge in their ideation process, such as lack of inspiration, difficulty validating ideas, or inefficiencies in the brainstorming process.

### Friction Points
- Limited resources and time for ideation
- Difficulty in identifying viable and innovative ideas
- Lack of a structured process for idea validation

### User Emotions
- Frustration
- Overwhelm
- Anxiety

### Opportunities to Delight
- Introducing a user-friendly, AI-powered tool that streamlines the ideation process
- Providing a structured approach to idea validation
- Offering a library of successful case studies and industry trends

### Metric
- Number of new users exposed to the tool

## Consider

### Trigger Event
Indie hacker researches potential solutions to their ideation challenges and discovers idea-luminate.

### Friction Points
- Understanding the unique value proposition of idea-luminate
- Comparing idea-luminate with other ideation tools in the market
- Evaluating the cost-effectiveness of the tool

### User Emotions
- Curiosity
- Skepticism
- Excitement

### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering a free trial or demo to showcase the tool's capabilities
- Providing clear and concise product demos and case studies
- Offering competitive pricing and flexible subscription plans

### Metric
- Number of free trials or demos requested
- Conversion rate from free trial to paid subscription

## Try

### Trigger Event
Indie hacker signs up for a free trial or demo of idea-luminate.

### Friction Points
- Learning curve associated with using the tool
- Technical issues during the trial period
- Difficulty in integrating idea-luminate with existing workflows

### User Emotions
- Determination
- Frustration (if technical issues arise)
- Hopefulness

### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering comprehensive user guides and tutorials
- Providing dedicated customer support during the trial period
- Ensuring a smooth and seamless integration with existing workflows

### Metric
- User engagement during the trial period (number of sessions, time spent, etc.)

## Adopt

### Trigger Event
Indie hacker decides to purchase a subscription to idea-luminate after the trial period.

### Friction Points
- Completing the subscription process
- Ensuring a smooth onboarding experience
- Addressing any lingering concerns or questions

### User Emotions
- Relief
- Confidence
- Excitement

### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering a seamless onboarding process
- Providing dedicated customer success managers for support
- Ensuring a positive first experience with the tool

### Metric
- Number of new subscribers

## Expand

### Trigger Event
Indie hacker begins to use idea-luminate regularly and considers expanding their usage.

### Friction Points
- Identifying additional use cases for idea-luminate
- Evaluating the ROI of expanding usage
- Deciding on additional features or services to purchase

### User Emotions
- Satisfaction
- Curiosity
- Ambition

### Opportunities to Delight
- Offering upsell opportunities based on the user's needs and usage patterns
- Providing additional features or services that address the user's expanding needs
- Offering tiered pricing plans to accommodate different levels of usage

### Metric
- Average revenue per user (ARPU)
- Churn rate (number of users who cancel their subscriptions)
